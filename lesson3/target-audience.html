Target Audience


The target audience for my page is people who are moving to, passing through, or visiting Helena, MT. They could be looking for information on what to do in the city, what restaurants are around, or just want some more information on the city and its history. That being said, the page will be geared towards American adults, both male and female.

Personas

Hannah loves the outdoors and just moved to Helena, MT. She is a 28 year old female. She wants to learn what activities there are to do around the community and wants more general information about her new home.

Mark is taking his family on a vacation this summer and they are staying in Helena, MT for a few days. He is 35 years old, married, with 2 kids. Him and his family enjoy trying out local restaurants and trying new things. He wants to find more information on kid-friendly activities around Helena as well as information on the best places to eat in the city.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: I am visiting Helena and am hungry but don't know any restaurants in this city. I'm going to use the Helena City Chamber of Commerce site so that I can get more information on local restaurants, then I'll pick where I'll eat.


Scenario 2: My family is going to move to Helena soon. We would like to figure out more about the city, and want a site where there is a lot of information in 1 spot. I'm going to use the Helena City Chamber site to laern about the city and get the information I need.


Scenario 3: I'm looking for some activitie to do around Helena, MT. I specifically want outdoors activity ideas. I'm going to use the Helena Chamber site to learn more about what there is to do in and around Helena, MT.


Scenario 4: I'm an avid skier and want to get more information on the ski hill close to Helena, MT to see if I should come visit. Using the information from the Helena City chamber site, I'll be able to see if I would like to go to the ski hill in Helena.


Scenario 5: I'm expanding my business to different cities and am thinking about expanding to Helena, MT. I want to learn more about the current businesses there and the community. The Helena City chamber site will provide me the information I need.
